Privacy Policy – BillSmart

Last updated: March 12, 2026

BillSmart ("the App") is developed by Astra AppWorks. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use BillSmart.

1. Information We Collect

BillSmart is an offline-first invoicing app. Most data (invoices, client details, business profile) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

However, the following data may be collected through third-party SDKs:

  • Device or Advertising ID – Collected by Google AdMob to display relevant advertisements.
  • Purchase Information – Managed by Google Play Billing for in-app purchases and subscriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions via Google Play.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob – For serving ads.
  • Google Play Billing – For in-app purchases.

4. Data Storage and Security

All invoice data, client data, and business profile information is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store this data on any server. Data is encrypted in transit when interacting with third-party services.

5. Children's Privacy

BillSmart is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Deletion

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. For advertising-related data managed by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's privacy controls.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
